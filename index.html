English in Kiswahili Word Explorer

Words found in this website are suggested and checked by both ai and a human mind (Mr. Gyavira Kamara & Mr. Alvin Mwasha). An interactive tool to discover and explore the rich vocabulary shared between the English and Swahili languages. Many Kiswahili words found here are borrowed from English. Other English words have been provided because of being common to many Kiswahili speakers. Arabic origin of Kiswahili words for common English ones is also acknowledged. Search for words or use the filters to see different types of linguistic connections
